The Banking Firewall: Separation of Debt and Income

Executive Summary (TL;DR)

  • The Threat: The "Right of Offset" allows banks to legally seize checking/savings funds without a court order if you default on their credit products.
  • The Strategy: Create a total separation of "Debt" and "Income" by using entirely different financial institutions.
  • The Goal: Force creditors to use the slow legal system (lawsuits) rather than instant internal fund transfers.

You haven't been hacked. You have been hit by the Right of Offset.

The Danger: Right of Offset
