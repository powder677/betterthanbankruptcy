About MoreThanBankruptcy.com

Your Net Worth Is Not
Your Self Worth.

If you are reading this, you have likely just walked through fire. Maybe you filed for Chapter 7. Maybe you settled massive debt. Maybe you simply hit "reset" on your financial life.

Whatever brought you here, you probably noticed something frustrating about the financial advice industry: Everyone has advice on how to fall—how to file papers, how to attend court hearings, how to liquidate assets.

Almost no one tells you how to get back up.

That is why we built MoreThanBankruptcy.com.

The "Day After" Problem

For millions of people, the day after their debt is discharged is the quietest, scariest day of their lives. The phone stops ringing, the creditors stop calling, and suddenly... you are alone. You have a credit score that feels permanently broken. You feel defined by a single legal status: "Bankrupt."

We believe that is wrong. We believe bankruptcy is a legal tool, not a character flaw. It is a line in the sand—a harsh ending, yes, but also a pristine beginning.

Our Mission

To shorten the time between your "Rock Bottom" and your "Bounce Back." We are a team of researchers, writers, and advocates providing the toolkit for the Day After.

What We Believe

Empathy Over Judgment

We do not care how you got into debt. We care about how you get out of it. No shame, only strategy.

Practicality

You don't need positive vibes. You need to know which secured card has the lowest fees. We focus on data.

Radical Transparency

The financial industry is full of sharks. We strive to be the lighthouse. We are open about how we make money.

A Note on Integrity & Standards

How We Make Money

MoreThanBankruptcy.com is reader-supported. When you click links to buy products or services we recommend, we may earn an affiliate commission.

How We Choose Products

We recommend products that pass our "Recovery Test": Would we recommend this to a friend starting over? If a product has predatory fees or confusing terms, we don't list it. Period.
