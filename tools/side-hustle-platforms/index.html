Detailed Reviews

1. Upwork

Best for Professionals

Upwork is the giant of the industry. It operates on a "bidding" model: clients post jobs, and you submit a proposal. It is best for people with tangible digital skills (writing, coding, admin, bookkeeping). In 2025, they shifted to a variable fee structure (0-15%), which can be cheaper for new freelancers than the old flat rate.
