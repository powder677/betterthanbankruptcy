How to Choose: The Decision Framework

The Hidden Cost: Registered Agents

Every LLC must have a Registered Agent (a person/business designated to receive legal mail). You can be your own, but your address becomes public.

  • Free Year Bizee and Northwest include this for free for the first year. This saves you ~$150.
  • Extra Cost ZenBusiness and LegalZoom charge extra for this (usually ~$199/yr) unless you buy a high-tier package.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start an LLC if I have a bankruptcy on record? ▼

Yes. Your personal bankruptcy does not legally prevent you from forming an LLC. However, funding it might be harder (since you can't get business loans easily). Focus on service-based businesses that require low capital.

Do I need an EIN? ▼

Yes. An EIN is like a Social Security Number for your business. You need it to open a bank account (like Novo). You can get it for free from the IRS website, or pay these services to get it for you.

Should I be my own Registered Agent? ▼

We recommend against it. If you list your home address, it becomes public record for marketers and creditors to find. Using a service keeps your personal life private.
