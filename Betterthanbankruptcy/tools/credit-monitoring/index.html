How to Choose: The Decision Framework

FICO® vs. VantageScore

This is the #1 source of confusion. Why does Credit Karma say you have a 680, but the bank says you have a 640?

  • FICO Used by 90% of lenders. It penalizes paid collections less heavily than older models, but heavily weighs utilization. Check this before applying for loans.
  • Vantage Created by the bureaus. It moves faster and is great for tracking "trends" (did I go up or down?), but the specific number is often higher than your FICO. Use this for daily monitoring.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does checking my own credit hurt my score? ▼

No. Checking your own credit via these apps is a "soft inquiry." It has zero impact on your score. You can check it daily.

Do I really need to pay for monitoring? ▼

Not necessarily. If you are just maintaining, Credit Karma is fine. But if you are actively rebuilding or have been a victim of identity theft, the insurance and 3-bureau accuracy of Aura or Experian are worth the cost.

How often do these scores update? ▼

Most paid services (Aura, Experian Premium) update daily. Free services (Credit Karma) usually update weekly or when there is a significant change on your report.
