How to Choose: The Decision Framework

Active Bidding vs. Passive Gigs

Upwork requires you to hunt. You must write proposals daily. It's active sales.

Fiverr is passive. You set up your shop and wait. It requires better marketing/SEO skills to get found.

Digital vs. Physical

If you have a computer and WiFi, Upwork/Fiverr are scalable.

If you have a car/truck and tools, TaskRabbit often pays a higher starting hourly rate, but it is physically exhausting and capped by your location.
