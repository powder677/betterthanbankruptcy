⚠ A Warning on "Survivor Bias"

Be careful of "gurus" on social media who claim they made $1M the month after bankruptcy. We verify our stories. If someone claims a number, we ask for receipts. Real recovery is slow, boring, and often frustrating. Do not compare your "Day 1" to someone else's "Year 5."

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these stories verified?

Yes. For our detailed Case Studies (like Mark), we review financial documents (redacted for privacy) to ensure the timeline and numbers are realistic.

Can I share my story anonymously?

Absolutely. The Debt Diaries section is 100% anonymous. We believe privacy is essential for honest financial discussions.

Do these people offer coaching?

Generally, no. We are a publisher, not a coaching platform. We share their stories to inspire you, not to sell you their mentorship.
